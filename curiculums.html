Click Here for Instructions on Using This Site

 

To help make your learning experience as enjoyable as possible, please familiarise yourself with the navigation system of the courseware before you start.

To show this text you clicked on where it said “Click Here for Instructions on Using This Site” above.  To hide this text, click on that area again.

The page you are on right now is the ‘Curriculums‘ page and contains the full index of the Learning Centre.  You can access this page from anywhere by clicking the ‘Curriculums‘ button on the menu at the top of every page.  If at any time you get a bit lost or confused about where you should be, jump right back here.  It’s also a great place to find subjects & classes you might want to revisit.

You will see in large print, below these instructions, the headings of the Introductory pages and the curriculums, eg ‘Introduction to Tolman UniverCity’, ‘Curriculum 1’ etc.  Below these headings is the Curriculum Content where you can see, and navigate to, each subject.

Go ahead and scroll down to the first main heading, Introduction to Tolman UniverCity.  Do you see that there are two ‘subjects’ under that heading?  ‘Orientation‘ and ‘About You‘ should be showing.  If you click on ‘Orientation‘ you will go straight to that page, but don’t go there just yet.  To the right of the Curriculum Content heading, in small print, is an option to ‘Expand All | Collapse All‘.  By clicking on ‘Expand All‘, the class titles for the subjects within that curriculum will appear.  Clicking on the class title will navigate to that class.

At the bottom of each class is either a Quiz for you to take, or a button that says ‘Mark Complete‘.  If there is a quiz you must complete it before you proceed, otherwise hitting the Mark Complete button will take you to the next class.  As you complete classes, a green dot will appear to the left of the class title on this page.  When you complete all the classes within a subject, the status to the right of the subject title will display a green tick (check mark).

If you have any problems with the navigation system, please use the ‘Contact Us‘ button at the top right of the page to let us know.  For now, click on ‘Orientation‘ below, and enjoy.
